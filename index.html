About the Order

The Mekong Order is an invitation-only club inspired by explorers’ societies, heritage foundations and gastronomic movements. We convene scholars, royalty, anthropologists, libertarians and slow food advocates to celebrate the Mekong region’s culture and landscapes.

Membership

Membership is by invitation only. Our members are passionate about land reform, liberty, exploration, culinary arts and cultural heritage. Prospective members are nominated by existing members.

Expeditions & Events

We organise lectures, excursions and dinners that explore the cultural and natural heritage of Southeast Asia, such as:

  • An expedition through Luang Prabang tracing Lan Xang’s royal history.
  • A symposium on land value tax featuring leading Georgist scholars.
  • A Slow Food dinner celebrating indigenous Lao ingredients.
  • An anthropological field study of highland minority cultures in northern Thailand.

Culinary Heritage

Our culinary events honour slow food traditions and regional delicacies, reflecting the abundance of the Mekong basin.
